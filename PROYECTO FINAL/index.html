GOOD DESIGN TRANSFORM SPACES, IMPROVE LIVES

Welcome to my architectural design portfolio!

Here, you'll find a reflection of my passion for creating spaces that not only meet functional needs but also inspire and connect people with their surroundings. Each project is an opportunity to explore new ideas, materials, and innovative solutions aimed at improving quality of life through design.

My approach is based on the belief that architecture has the power to transform and enrich the human experience. Whether in residential, commercial, or urban projects, my goal is always to create environments that are not only aesthetically impactful but also sustainable, functional, and deeply connected to their context.

I hope you enjoy exploring my work and find the inspiration you're looking for for your own projects. Thank you for visiting!

ABOUT ME

Castañeda Cintia

I am an architect passionate about innovative design and the creation of functional, sustainable spaces.

I have experience in planning, designing, and supervising architectural projects, ranging from residential housing to commercial and industrial spaces. My approach combines creativity, attention to detail, and a solid understanding of construction principles, aiming to deliver solutions that are not only aesthetically appealing but also practical and efficient.

Throughout my career, I have developed advanced skills in design tools such as AutoCAD, Revit, and SketchUp, as well as in leading interdisciplinary teams to ensure each project meets quality standards and deadlines. Additionally, I have a strong interest in sustainable architecture, incorporating eco-friendly materials and design techniques that minimize environmental impact. I strive to communicate effectively with clients and collaborators, ensuring that project ideas and objectives translate into successful outcomes. I am committed to continuous learning, staying up to date with architectural trends and emerging technologies to enhance my skills and contribute to projects that inspire and transform.
